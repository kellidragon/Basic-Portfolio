About Me

I feel that I am most fulfilled when working on creative projects. After studying film production in college I found myself working on various tv and film sets for major networks. During my time working in production I was built by this persistent bug, the travel bug. I decided if I was going to seriously travel it was going to be now or never. So, I took a job in the Middle East and made traveling my main priority. For the next three years I was fortunate enough to step foot on about 80 different countries. I consider myself a curious person who loves challenges that require me to dabble into different things.
